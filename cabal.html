The Cabal

The Cabal are a militaristic, empire-driven species that operate under a strict chain of command. They are massive, heavily-armored humanoids with powerful weaponry and advanced technology. Originally from their homeworld, Torobatl, they arrived in the Solar System centuries ago and have since occupied Mars, Nessus, and various other planets.

The Cabal Empire was once ruled by Emperor Calus, but he was overthrown by Dominus Ghaul, who led the Red Legion in an invasion of the Last City. Following Ghaul's defeat, the Cabal splintered into multiple factions, some remaining loyal to the Empire while others followed new warlords and conspirators.

Despite their setbacks, the Cabal remain a formidable force, now allied with humanity under Empress Caiatl in the fight against the Witness.

Infantry

War Beast

War Beasts are vicious, quadrupedal creatures used by the Cabal as attack hounds. They attack in packs and can quickly overwhelm their enemies.

Legionary

Legionaries are the backbone of the Cabal military, wielding powerful slug rifles. They are heavily armored and disciplined fighters.

Phalanx

Phalanxes are Cabal infantry that carry massive shields, using them for both defense and melee attacks. Their energy shields can deflect most frontal assaults.

Centurion

Centurions are elite Cabal officers equipped with jetpacks and powerful energy weapons. They are more resilient and can regenerate their shields after taking damage.

Colossus

Colossi are towering Cabal warriors equipped with Gatling guns and missile launchers. They are slow but have immense firepower and endurance.

Gladiator

Gladiators are melee specialists that wield massive twin cleavers. They charge aggressively and can take down enemies in just a few hits.

Emplacements

Goliath Tank

The Goliath Tank is a massive, heavily-armored Cabal vehicle with multiple heavy cannons. It is a powerful force on the battlefield, used for siege operations.

Thresher

Thresher gunships are fast, aerial assault vehicles equipped with rapid-fire cannons and missile pods. They provide air support during battles.

Land Tank

Land Tanks are colossal mobile fortresses, acting as command centers for Cabal legions. They are outfitted with artillery, energy shields, and defensive turrets.
