The Hive

The Hive are an ancient, monstrous species that worship the Darkness and exist in a state of eternal war. Their entire society is built upon the Sword Logic, a philosophy that rewards strength and destruction. They inhabit massive underground fortresses called Dreadnaughts, as well as the depths of the Moon, Titan, Savath√ªn's Throne World, and other hidden places across the galaxy.

The Hive trace their origins to the gas giant Fundament, where they were once a fragile race known as the Krill. Through a pact with the Worm Gods, they transformed into the Hive and spread across the cosmos, consuming entire worlds to feed their endless hunger for power.

The Hive are ruled by a hierarchy of gods, princes, and wizards, each serving under the strongest among them. Their devotion to the Darkness makes them one of the most formidable threats in the universe.

Infantry

Thrall

Thralls are mindless, clawed creatures that rush their enemies in overwhelming numbers. They lack armor but attack with relentless fury.

Acolyte

Acolytes are the rank-and-file soldiers of the Hive, wielding energy weapons and summoning deadly traps. They are more intelligent than Thralls and work in coordinated units.

Knight

Knights are heavily armored Hive warriors that wield massive swords, boomerangs, or projectile weapons. They serve as frontline enforcers in Hive armies.

Wizard

Wizards are powerful spellcasters that use arcane energy to summon minions and unleash devastating elemental attacks. They are highly mobile and difficult to kill due to their ability to regenerate health.

Ogre

Ogres are enormous, nightmarish beasts that channel void energy through their single, glowing eye. They are extremely powerful and highly resistant to damage.

Emplacements

Shrieker

Shriekers are floating, organic turrets that fire deadly homing projectiles. They are usually found guarding Hive strongholds.

Tombship

Tombships are Hive warships that phase in and out of existence, allowing them to teleport reinforcements directly onto the battlefield.

Ritual Circle

Ritual Circles are Hive constructs that act as conduits for dark magic, used to summon reinforcements, empower Hive warriors, or perform occult rituals.
